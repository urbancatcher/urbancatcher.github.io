Urbancatcher is a doodler.


Born in the 80s and bred in Hong Kong, Ur's work orbits around her encounter with the sureal urban life. Ur travels from cities to cities and can be found mostly in Salt Lake City and Hong Kong.


Ur loves doodling so much that she can be seen doodling everywhere: in a crowded subway, on a street corner or even during a gig! She uses both digital and traditional mediums to create illustrations and graphics inspired by the things she has seen and experienced.
